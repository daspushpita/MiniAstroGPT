Task: Given a research abstract and an ID, produce a single valid JSON object with exactly two keys:
"id" and "explanation".

	1.	Completely rewrite the abstract from scratch. Do not reuse its phrasing or sentence structure.

"we present", "we show", "we find", "we propose", "we investigate",
"this paper", "this work", "the authors", "the study", "our results",
"we employ", "we define".
	3.	Do NOT refer to researchers, authors, papers, studies, methods, or results presentation.
	4.	Write as if directly explaining how the physical phenomena work, not discussing a document.
	5.	Use third-person, non-personalized language. Never use "we", "they", or references to people.
	6.	Define every technical term the first time it appears, in plain language.
	7.	No equations, symbols, variables, or math.
	8.	Tone should be warm, educational, and intuitive, similar to a high-quality science magazine article.

• Begin with the big-picture physical context and why the topic matters.
• Introduce and clearly explain all key technical concepts needed to understand the topic (only those appearing in the abstract).
• Explain the underlying physical mechanism or reasoning in intuitive terms.
• Explicitly state the specific scientific claims made in this abstract, in plain language.
• Conclude with limitations and implications, why the effects are small or hard to observe, and what this means for our understanding of the universe.

• Stay strictly faithful to the scientific claims in the abstract.
• Do not add new mechanisms, entities, theories, or speculation.
• Background information is allowed only if it directly supports understanding of the abstract.
• Do not summarize the abstract; explain the ideas behind it.
• The explanation should prioritize intuition, background, and meaning over listing conclusions.

OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS:

• Output ONLY a single valid JSON object.
• No markdown, no commentary, no extra text.
• Your response MUST start with {{ and end with }}.
• Use this exact schema:

{{"id":"{paper_id}","explanation":"..."}}
